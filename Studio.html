The Studio

Located in the outskirts of Copenhagen, we're only a few stops with the train from the main central station. Well isolated from the busy city, we create a relaxed atmosphere in the studio, consisting of a control room, equipped state of the art recording hardware and software, and a live room of approximentely 20 square meters, with plenty of room to setup a full live band. You're able to kick back and relax in the lounge area in between takes, and treat yourself to a nice cup of coffe.
